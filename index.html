Emergency Question?

If you're experiencing a dental emergency, please call us immediately.

(727) 443-5514

What makes your practice different?

With over 30 years of experience, we emphasize proven techniques and a steady, reliable approach. We're known for low wait times, friendly staff, clear explanations, and our commitment to painless dentistry using modern science and techniques.

Do you handle dental emergencies?

Yes! Dr. Eifert is known for accommodating patients in emergency situations (such as dental trauma from accidents) without excessive charges. We provide compassionate emergency care to help you feel comfortable during stressful situations.

What is "painless dentistry"?

Painless dentistry refers to our focus on utilizing modern science and techniques to minimize the pain and time typically associated with older dental methods. This includes advanced numbing techniques, digital imaging for faster diagnosis, and comfortable treatment approaches.

What types of fillings do you offer?

We offer multiple material options for restorative fillings, including tooth-colored composites. The choice depends on each patient's specific needs, location of the filling, and personal preferences for appearance and durability.

Do you accept dental insurance?

Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans. Our office will work with you to maximize your insurance benefits and provide clear explanations of any out-of-pocket costs. We also offer payment plans for larger treatments.
